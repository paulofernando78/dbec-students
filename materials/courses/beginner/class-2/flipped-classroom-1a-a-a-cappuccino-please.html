Grammar


VERB BE (SINGULAR): I and you


Listen the positive and negative examples.



(+)

I am Helen.

I'm Helen.


You are Tom.

You're Tom.


(-)

I am not Helen.

I'm not Helen.


You are not Dom.

You aren't Dom.

I am not -> I'm not
