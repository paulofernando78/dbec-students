PRESENTATION
(Grammar)


Meaning


Listen up!

Ouça! / Ouçam!


Introducing yourself to someone

Se apresentando para alguém



Listen to the dialogue.

Ouça / Ouçcam o diálogo.




A: Hello. Are you Helen?

B: Yes, I am. And you're Tom. Yes! nice to meet you.

A: Nice to meet you.



Pronunciation


Repeat after me.


Are you... ? Você é...

I'm not... ?

And you're... ? E você é...

Nice to meet you. Prazer em conhecê-lo(a).


Form / Usage


I and you + verb be


Listen the positive and negative examples.




(+)

I am Helen.

I 'm Helen.


you are Tom.

you're Tom.


Subject + verb be

(-)

I am not Helen.

I'm not Helen.


you are not Dom.

you aren't Dom.


Subject + verb be (not)

PRODUCTION
(Grammar)



Introduce yourself to your classmate.

Apresente-se ao seu colega de classe.



Example 1

A: Hi. I'm (name). Nice to meet you.

B: Hello. I'm ("B" name). Nice to meet you, too.


Example 2

A: What's your name?

B: My name's... . Nice to meet you. What's your name?

A: I'm... Nice to meet you, too.


Example 3

A: Hello. Are you Helen?

B: Yes, I am. And you're (A's name). Yes! Nice to meet you.
