Unit 45 Who saw you? / Who did you see?




A


Look at the picture.



see present - saw past


Silvia saw Paul.

Silvia = subject = she.

Nesse caso, quando nos referimos ao autor da ação (pronome sujeito), não usamos auxiliares para fazer perguntas.

Who saw Paul? Silvia.

"Somebody" = Silvia saw Paul.


Silvia saw Paul.

Paul = object = him.

Who did Silvia see? She saw Paul.

Agora, quando estamos falando de quem recebeu a ação (pronome objeto), usamos auxiliares para fazer perguntas.

Silvia saw Paul = "somebody".


Pronouns

Subject / Object

I -> me

you -> you

he -> him

she -> her

it -> it

we -> us

you -> you

they -> them



B


Compare:

• George likes oranges.

Who likes oranges? George. (Subject)

What does George like? Oranges (Object)


• Jane won a new car.

Who won a new car? Jane. (Subject)

What did Jane win? A new car. (Object)


In these questions, who/what is the subject:

• Who lives in this house? John lives in it.

• What happened? The truck broke down.

• What's happening? "Something" is happening.

• Who has my key? Mary has the key. *Who's got my key?


In these questions, who/what is the object:

• Who did you meet yesterday? You met "somebody".

• What did Paul say? Paul said "something".

• Who are you phoning? I'm phoning "somebody".

• What was Silvia wearing? She was wearing "something".




C


Use who for people (somebody). Use what for things, ideas.

• Who's your favorite singer?

• What's your favorite song?
