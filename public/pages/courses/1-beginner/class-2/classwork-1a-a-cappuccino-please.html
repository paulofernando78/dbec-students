PRESENTATION
(Grammar)


Meaning


Listen up!

Ouça! / Ouçam!


Introducing yourself to someone

Se apresentando para alguém



Listen to the dialogue.

Ouça / Ouçcam o diálogo.




A: Hello. Are you Helen?

B: Yes, I am. And you're Tom. Yes! nice to meet you.

A: Nice to meet you.



Pronunciation


Repeat after me.


Are you... ? Você é...

I'm not... ?

And you're... ? E você é...

Nice to meet you. Prazer em conhecê-lo(a).


Form / Usage


I and you + verb be


Listen the positive and negative examples.




(+)

I am Helen.

I 'm Helen.


you are Tom.

you're Tom.


Subject + verb be

(-)

I am not Helen.

I'm not Helen.


you are not Dom.

you aren't Dom.


Subject + verb be (not)


Notes


WRONG ERRADO

I amn't.

i'm Helen. "I" (sempre em maiúsculo)


Listen the interrogative (question) examples.




(?)

A: Am I in room 2?

B: Yes, you are / No, you aren't.


A: Are you Mike?

B: Yes, I am. / No, I 'm not.


Am I John?

Am I a student?

Are you... ?
