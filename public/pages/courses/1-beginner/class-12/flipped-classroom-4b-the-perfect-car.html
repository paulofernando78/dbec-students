Adjectives


4.19 • Listen the examples. Then, read the rules. regras




1. An Audi is expensive. It's fast.

2. An Audi is an expensive car. It's a fast car.

3. They're old houses. My glasses are new.

4. He's tall. She's tall, too.




1. We use adjectives after the verb be, e.g.,

An Audi is expensive.

An Audi expensive is.


2. We use adjectives before a noun, e.g.,

It's a fast car.

It's a fast car.


3. Adjectives are the same for singular and plural:

It's an old house. They're old houses.

They're olds houses.


4. Adjectives are the same for masculine and feminine.

He's a tall man.

She's a tall woman.
