FUTURE FORMS


Future plans and intentions (be going to + base form)


We use be going to when we have already decided to do something.


Examples

My sister's gonna adopt a child.

Are you gonna buy a new car or a used one?

I'm not gonna go to New York City tomorrow. The meeting is canceled.


Predictions (be going to + base form)


We also use be going to to make a prediction about the future, especially when we have some evidence.


Examples

The Yankees are gonna win. They're playing really well.

Look at those black clouds. I think it's gonna rain.


When something is about to happen.


Example

Get back! The bomb is gonna explode. (The bomb is ticking)


Future arrangements (present continuous: be + verb + -ing)


We often use the present continuous for future arrangements. There is a very little difference between the present continuous and be going to for future plans / arrangements, and often you can use either.


• be going to shows that you have made a decision.


Example

We're going to get married next year.


• the present continouos emphasizes that you have made the arrangements.


Example

We're getting married on October 12th (twelfth).

* We've already ordered the invitations, etc.

Já encomendamos os convites, etc.


More examples

Lorna and James are getting married in October.

We're meeting at 10:00 tomorrow in Jack's office.

Jane's leaving on Friday and coming back next Tuesday.


Will + base form


We use will / won't for:


Instant decision


Example

I'll have the steak (at a restaurant).

I'm thirsty. I think I'll buy a drink.


Promises


Example

I won't tell anybody where you are, don't worry.

I'll behave next time, I promise.


Offers


Example

I'll carry that bag for you.

That box looks heavy. I'll help you with it.


Personal predictions


Example

I think the Yankees will win.

You'll love New York City!


Future facts


Example

The election will be on March 1st.

I'll be home all afternoon.

I'll drive to work tomorrow, if you want a ride.


Refusal Recusa


Example

She won't listen to anything I say.
